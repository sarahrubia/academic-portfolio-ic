I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Federal University of Minas Gerais (UFMG), Brazil. My research interests rely on Data Analysis and Artificial Intelligence, especially using technology to solve social problems. Currently, I am studying how to apply Neural Architecture Search on Federated Learning scenarios.

In Python, I am fluent in different data processing libraries, such as scikit-learn, numpy, pandas, keras, sktime, and networkx.
